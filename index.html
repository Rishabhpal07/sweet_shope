gulab jamun

ingredients

  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • All-purpose Flour (Maida)
  • Sugar Syrup

recipe

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar and water with cardamom until it reaches a sticky, single-thread consistency. Set aside and keep warm.
  2. Make Dough and Fry: Knead khoya, maida, and baking powder into a smooth dough. Shape into small balls and fry in ghee on low heat until golden brown.
  3. Soak and Serve: Add the fried gulab jamuns to the warm syrup and let them soak for at least 2 hours before serving. Enjoy!

barfi

ingredients

  • Milk or Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

recipe

  1. Prepare Base: Heat ghee in a pan, add milk or milk powder, and cook until it thickens, stirring continuously.
  2. Sweeten and Flavor: Add sugar and cardamom, and cook until the mixture becomes soft and leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Set and Serve: Pour into a greased tray, flatten, add nuts if desired, and let it cool. Cut into squares once set. Enjoy!
